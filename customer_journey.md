## 3. Key Changes for Launch Phase

- **No separate Document Controller:** Sales Consultants (Bashar, Mahfoud) are responsible for document collection and verification.
- **No separate Operations Officer:** Sales Consultants (Bashar, Mahfoud) are also responsible for application processing and managing external vendors.
- **Clear CSR language split:** Zain handles English-speaking clients, and Eman handles Arabic-speaking clients.
- **Internal PRO for Labor:** Eman handles all services related to MOHRE and Labor.
- **Shared responsibility:** Bashar and Mahfoud share the load for Sales, Operations, and Vendor Management.
