**Legend:**
- **R** = Responsible (Does the work)
- **A** = Accountable (Owns the work)
- **C** = Consulted (Provides input)
- **I** = Informed (Kept up-to-date)

## 4. Success Criteria for Month 1

At the end of the 30-day launch phase, the following metrics will be evaluated to decide on hiring and expansion:

- **Lead to Conversion Rate:** > 20%
- **Average Client Onboarding Time:** < 5 business days
- **Client Satisfaction Score (CSAT):** > 4.5 / 5
- **Team Workload:** Is any member consistently exceeding 100% capacity?
- **Revenue Target:** Achieve AED 50,000 in booked revenue.

If these criteria are met, the full organizational structure will be implemented.
